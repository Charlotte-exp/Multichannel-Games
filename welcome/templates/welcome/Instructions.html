The Game

Each game lasts for at least 20 rounds. After that, there is a ....% chance of another round (explain that better)

The game structure is as follows: At each round, both players get a choice of two actions: cooperate or defect. Explain the matrix payoffs

The colours signals the different players.

  • Blue signals player 1
  • Red signals player 2

The game ends on the last round, which ever it is. You have no way of knowing for sure. One round will then be randomly selected and you will be paid that amount.

After you press the next button, the game will start only after the other player has arrived.
